Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Data Collection

YEET collects the following data to help you track your flips:

  • Purchase and sale information for items you're flipping
  • Expense records (amounts, categories, dates, vendors)
  • Photos you take or select for documentation
  • Notes and descriptions you enter

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device and synced to your personal iCloud account using CloudKit. YEET does not have access to your data - it lives entirely in your iCloud storage.

Data Sharing

YEET does not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Your flip data is yours alone.
